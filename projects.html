Lateral Flow Assay for Drug-Facilitated Sexual Assault Prevention

2022 - 2023

Nanotechnology Chemical Sensing Lateral Flow Assay

We designed and prototyped a portable test for gamma hydroxybutyric acid (GHB) in beverages, which is one of the most common drugs used in drug-facilitated sexual assault. The mechanism leveraged bifunctionalized silver nanoparticles and gave a colorimetric result.

Recognition: This project won first place in the Molecular & Tissue Engineering and Drug Discovery category of the first annual BTEC x BMES Design-a-thon, which aims to address health disparities.
